二重积分​

性质公式
区域面积A=∬DdσA=\iint_{D}d\sigmaA=∬D​dσ
可积有界∬Df(x,y)dσ=A  ⟹  m≤f(x,y)≤M\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}\sigma=A\implies m\le f\left(x,y\right)\le M∬D​f(x,y)dσ=A⟹m≤f(x,y)≤M
保号性∥∬Df(x,y)dσ∥≤∬D∥f(x,y)∥dσ\left\|\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}\sigma\right\|\le\iint_{D_{}}\left\|f\left(x,y\right)\right\|\mathrm{d}\sigma​∬D​f(x,y)dσ​≤∬D​​∥f(x,y)∥dσ
估值定理mA≤∬Df(x,y)dσ≤MAmA\le\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)d\sigma\le MAmA≤∬D​f(x,y)dσ≤MA
线性可拆∬D[k1f(x,y)+k2g(x,y)]dσ=k1∬Df(x,y)dσ+k2∬Dg(x,y)dσ\iint_{D}\left\lbrack k_1f\left(x,y\right)+k_2g\left(x,y\right)]\mathrm{d}\sigma\right.=k_1\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}\sigma+k_2\iint_{D}g\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}\sigma∬D​[k1​f(x,y)+k2​g(x,y)]dσ=k1​∬D​f(x,y)dσ+k2​∬D​g(x,y)dσ
区域可加∬Df(x,y)dσ=∬D1f(x,y)dσ+∬D2f(x,y)dσ\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}\sigma=\iint_{D_1}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}\sigma+\iint_{D_2}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}\sigma∬D​f(x,y)dσ=∬D1​​f(x,y)dσ+∬D2​​f(x,y)dσ
中值定理∬Df(x,y)dσ=f(ξ,η)A\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}\sigma=f\left(\xi,\eta\right)A∬D​f(x,y)dσ=f(ξ,η)A
普通对称∬Df(x,y)dxdy={2∬D1f(x,y)dxdyf(−x,y)=f(x,y)0,f(x,y)=−f(−x,y)\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{dxdy}=\left\{\begin{array}{}2\iint_{D_1}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y &f\left(-x,y\right)=f\left(x,y\right)\\ 0,& f(x,y)=-f(-x,y)\end{array}\right.∬D​f(x,y)dxdy={2∬D1​​f(x,y)dxdy0,​f(−x,y)=f(x,y)f(x,y)=−f(−x,y)​
轮换对称∬Df(x,y)dσ=∬Df(y,x)dσ\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)d\sigma=\iint_{D}f\left(y,x\right)d\sigma∬D​f(x,y)dσ=∬D​f(y,x)dσ

二重积分计算方法​

直角坐标系：∬Df(x,y)dσ=∫abdx∫φ1(x)φ2(x)f(x,y)dy=∫cddy∫ψ1(y)ψ2(y)f(x,y)dx\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)d\sigma=\int_{a}^{b}dx\int_{\varphi_1\left(x\right)}^{\varphi_2\left(x\right)}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}y=\int_{c}^{d}dy\int_{\psi_1\left(y\right)}^{\psi_2\left(y\right)}f\left(x,y\right)\mathrm{d}x∬D​f(x,y)dσ=∫ab​dx∫φ1​(x)φ2​(x)​f(x,y)dy=∫cd​dy∫ψ1​(y)ψ2​(y)​f(x,y)dx

极坐标系：∬Df(x,y)dσ=∫αβdθ∫r1(θ)r2(θ)f(rcos⁡θ,rsin⁡θ)rdr\iint_{D}f\left(x,y\right)d\sigma=\int_{\alpha}^{\beta}d\theta\int_{r_1\left(\theta\right)}^{r_2\left(\theta\right)}f\left(r\cos\theta,r\sin\theta\right)rdr∬D​f(x,y)dσ=∫αβ​dθ∫r1​(θ)r2​(θ)​f(rcosθ,rsinθ)rdr

三重积分​

权重空间，可以把看作是密度ρ\rhoρ

性质公式
物体体积∭Ωdv=V\iiint_{\Omega}dv=V∭Ω​dv=V
可积有界∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv=A  ⟹  m≤f(x,y,z)≤M\iiint_{\Omega}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv}=A\implies m\le f\left(x,y,z\right)\le M∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv=A⟹m≤f(x,y,z)≤M
保号性∥∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv∥≤∭Ω∥f(x,y,z)∥dv∥∥∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv≤∭Ωg(x,y,z)dv\left\|\iiint_{\Omega}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv}\right\|\le\iiint_{\Omega}\left\|f\left(x,y,z\right)\right\|\mathrm{dv}\quad \|\| \quad \iiint_\Omega f(x,y,z)dv \le\iiint_\Omega g(x,y,z)dv​∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv​≤∭Ω​∥f(x,y,z)∥dv∥∥∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv≤∭Ω​g(x,y,z)dv
估值定理mV≤∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv≤MVmV\le\iiint_{\Omega}f\left(x,y,z\right)dv\le MVmV≤∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv≤MV
线性可拆∭Ω[k1f(x,y,z)+k2g(x,y,z)]dv=k1∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv+k2∭Ωg(x,y,z)dv\iiint_{\Omega}\left\lbrack k_1f\left(x,y,z\right)+k_2g\left(x,y,z\right)]\mathrm{dv}\right.=k_1\iiint_{\Omega}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv}+k_2\iiint_{\Omega}g\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv}∭Ω​[k1​f(x,y,z)+k2​g(x,y,z)]dv=k1​∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv+k2​∭Ω​g(x,y,z)dv
区域可加∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv=∭Ω1f(x,y,z)dv+∭Ω2f(x,y,z)dv\iiint_{\Omega}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv}=\iiint_{\Omega_1}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv}+\iiint_{\Omega_2}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv}∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv=∭Ω1​​f(x,y,z)dv+∭Ω2​​f(x,y,z)dv
中值定理∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv=f(ξ,η,ζ)A\iiint_{\Omega}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv}=f\left(\xi,\eta,\zeta\right)A∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv=f(ξ,η,ζ)A
普通对称∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv={2∭Ω1f(x,y,z)dvf(−x,y,z)=f(x,y,z)0,f(x,y,z)=−f(−x,y,z)\iiint_{\Omega}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv}=\left\{\begin{array}{l}2\iiint_{\Omega_1}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{dv} & f\left(-x,y,z\right)=f\left(x,y,z\right)\\ 0,&f(x,y,z)=-f(-x,y,z)\end{array}\right.∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv={2∭Ω1​​f(x,y,z)dv0,​f(−x,y,z)=f(x,y,z)f(x,y,z)=−f(−x,y,z)​
轮换对称∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv=∭Ωf(y,x,z)dv\iiint_{\Omega}f\left(x,y,z\right)\mathrm{d}v= \iiint_{\Omega}f\left(y,x,z\right)\mathrm{d}v∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv=∭Ω​f(y,x,z)dv

  2. 投影穿线法:∭Ωf(x,y,z)dv=∬Dxydσ∫z1(x,y)z2(x,y)f(x,y,z)dz\iiint_{\Omega}f\left(x,y,z\right)dv=\iint_{D_{xy}}d\sigma\int_{z_1\left(x,y\right)}^{z_2\left(x,y\right)}f\left(x,y,z\right)dz∭Ω​f(x,y,z)dv=∬Dxy​​dσ∫z1​(x,y)z2​(x,y)​f(x,y,z)dz
